# Technical Security Assessment & Attack Exposure Review

## Overview

Cybersecurity incidents rarely begin with sophisticated attacks. Most compromises occur because of a small number of overlooked weaknesses such as weak access controls, missing multi-factor authentication, excessive privileges, or insecure remote access.

Our Technical Security Assessment & Attack Exposure Review is designed to help organizations understand how an attacker could realistically compromise their environment and what steps should be taken to reduce risk.

Rather than focusing on compliance checklists, we identify practical attack paths, technical weaknesses, and prioritized remediation actions.

---

## Assessment Objectives

The assessment is designed to:

* Identify technical security weaknesses
* Review security controls and configurations
* Evaluate exposure to common attack techniques
* Analyze potential attack paths
* Prioritize security improvements
* Provide actionable remediation recommendations

---

## Assessment Activities

The assessment may include:

### Identity & Access Review

* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Administrative privileges
* User access controls
* Privileged account management
* Account lifecycle management

### Microsoft 365 / Cloud Review

* Administrative roles
* Conditional Access policies
* Authentication controls
* Security configuration review
* External sharing settings

### Endpoint Security Review

* Endpoint protection coverage
* Device management controls
* Security monitoring capabilities
* Patch management practices

### Remote Access Review

* VPN security
* Remote workforce exposure
* Authentication controls
* Third-party access

### Exposure Analysis

* External attack surface review
* Public-facing services
* Internet-exposed systems
* Common attacker entry points

### Attack Path Analysis

We evaluate realistic attack scenarios that could impact the organization, including:

* Account compromise
* Business email compromise
* Ransomware
* Privilege escalation
* Unauthorized access to sensitive information

---

## Deliverables

### Executive Summary

A high-level overview of the organization's security posture and key areas of concern.

### Technical Findings

Each finding includes:

* Description
* Risk Rating
* Business Impact
* Technical Impact
* Recommended Remediation

### Attack Exposure Analysis

A review of the most likely attack paths affecting the organization, including:

* Initial access methods
* Potential attacker actions
* Business impact
* Mitigation recommendations

### Remediation Roadmap

Prioritized recommendations categorized by:

* Immediate Actions (0–30 Days)
* Near-Term Improvements (30–90 Days)
* Long-Term Enhancements (90+ Days)
